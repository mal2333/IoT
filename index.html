zapier set up link

  • exit_to_appTrigger
    • appsApp + Event

      The app's developpers have specified in advance what event in their app can trigger an action.

    • person_addAccount

      You are giving access to Zapier to access your account on that specific app.

    • notificationsTrigger

      A trigger can be anything from an incoming email to a new follower, those are predetermined by the app's developpers.

    • verified_userTest

      Zapier will now grab a previous event from your account to check if the connection has been made.
      From the test, you can view all the data that is being exchanged by the platforms.

  • Action - Adafruit.io
    • appsApp + Event

      Adafruit.io's developpers have specified in advance what event can be actioned by a trigger.

    • person_addAccount

      You are allowing Zapier to access your Adafruit.io account.

    • notificationsAction

      The action here is logging in a value to a feed key.

    • verified_userTest

      Zapier will now send the data (value) from your trigger above to your account to check if the connection has been made.
      From the test, you can view all the data that is being exchanged by the platforms.
